Once The Quiz Starts, these are the Rules:
  • 1. With each question you are given 15 seconds to answer.
  • 2. You have one shot to answer correctly, all answers are final.
  • 3. Once you run out of time, you can not select answers.
  • 4. Once you enter; you can not exit, until the quiz is completed.
  • 5. Points are acquired by answering the questions correctly.
